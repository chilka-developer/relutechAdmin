Purchase Leaseback

Relutech offers a Purchase Leaseback solution where we buy IT assets and then lease them back to the customer during the length of the migration. This provides a cash infusion upfront while ensuring critical infrastructure is maintained to bridge the gap between on-premises to the cloud. This solution creates a more predictable financial outcome, removes these assets from the balance sheet, and provides a with locked-in residual value.

Carbon Footprint Reduction

By eliminating datacenter equipment and moving to the cloud, companies can substantially reduce their carbon footprint. For these estimates, we made some fairly broad, but conservative assumptions. We compared the energy consumption of average servers running on-premises and in AWS in the United States, and extrapolated the total savings based on the migration timeline and total number of servers. For our calculations, we relied on the following research: Lannelongue, L., Grealey, J., Inouye, M., Green Algorithms: Quantifying the Carbon Footprint of Computation. Adv. Sci. 2021, 2100707.
